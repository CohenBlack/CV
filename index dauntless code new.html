Legendary Pikes Unique Abilities

Agarus Pike

This is a legendary weapon forged from the drops of the Terra boss Agarus with its unique ability being when activated it can heal and revive all slayers in a certain radius including yourself.

Malkarions Pike

This legendary weapon forged from the drops of the Electric Boss Malkarion. Its unique ability allows the slayer to teleport to the nearest behemoth unleashing a shock wave on impact.

Urskas Pike

This legendary weapon is forged from the drops of the Ice Boss Urska. Its unique ability allows the slayer to place a large ice shield in front of them blocking all projectiles. If a behemoth runs into the ice shield it gets interrupted meaning it falls over.

Chronovores Pike

This legendary weapon forged from the drops of the Radinat Light Boss The Chronovore. Its unquie ability allows the slayer to cheat death when defeated. the slayers leaps back in time 5 seconds and gains health and boosts lost and grants the player invincible for a short time.

Thrax's Pike

This legendary weapon forged from the drops of the Umbral Dark Boss Thrax. Its unique ability allows the slayer to jump into the sky and preform a massive ground slam attack damaging anything in that radius.

Torgadoros Pike

This legendary weapon forged from the drops of the Fire Boss Torgadoro. Its unique ability Turns the slayer into a rage mode which gives the player fire gauntlets which they can lightly ground slam and do punch attacks. While doing these moves fire and lava is thrown everwhere damaging behemoths and causing them to burn.
